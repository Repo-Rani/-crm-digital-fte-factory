# TechFlow Pro Product Documentation

## Getting Started
Welcome to TechFlow Pro, the ultimate command center for modern project management and team collaboration. This comprehensive guide is designed to take you from a new user to a power user, ensuring you leverage every feature of TechFlow Pro to drive your team's productivity and success. To begin your journey, navigate to the TechFlow login page and click the prominent 'Sign Up' button. You will be prompted to enter your work email address—we recommend using your primary professional email to ensure seamless integration with your team's existing workflows. After creating a strong, secure password that meets our complexity requirements, you will receive a verification email. Click the link in that email to activate your account.

Once verified, the first step is to name your primary workspace. A workspace in TechFlow Pro is the highest-level container, serving as a unified environment for all your projects, files, and team communication. Think of it as your virtual office. After naming your workspace, you can begin the onboarding process by inviting your colleagues. Simply enter their email addresses in the invitation dialog, and they will receive personalized links to join your workspace. You can assign them initial roles during this step, which can be modified later.

Your first project, titled 'My First Project', is created automatically to give you a sandbox to explore. You can rename this project to something more relevant to your current work. TechFlow Pro's interface is designed for intuitive navigation; the sidebar provides quick access to your projects, team members, and personal settings. To create your first task, simply click the '+' button within any column of your project board. Each task can be enriched with a title, a detailed description, an assignee, and a due date. We highly recommend starting with a classic Kanban structure—'To-Do', 'In Progress', and 'Done'—to visualize your workflow effectively. As you become more comfortable, you can explore advanced features like custom fields, automated workflows, and multi-project views. If you ever need to adjust your profile, security settings, or notification preferences, your personal avatar in the bottom-left corner is your gateway to account management. Here, you can also enable two-factor authentication (2FA), which we strongly advise for all users to maintain the highest level of data security.

## Task Management
Task management in TechFlow Pro is built to be both powerful and flexible, accommodating a wide range of project methodologies from simple task lists to complex agile workflows. Every task is more than just a line item; it's a rich data object that captures the full context of a piece of work. To dive into a task, simply click on its title to open the task detail pane.

In this pane, you can set the **Priority Level** of the task. TechFlow Pro offers four distinct levels: Low, Medium, High, and Urgent. High and Urgent tasks are automatically flagged with distinct icons (a yellow warning sign and a red exclamation mark, respectively) in all views, ensuring that critical items are never overlooked. Assigning accountability is straightforward; you can add one or more **Assignees** to a task. While we recommend having a single owner for clarity, multi-assignee support is available for collaborative tasks.

**Due Dates** are the heartbeat of project tracking. TechFlow Pro allows you to set both a start date and an end date, enabling precise timeline management and dependency tracking. For simpler tasks, a single deadline is often sufficient. If a task becomes too large, you can use **Subtasks** to break it down into smaller, more manageable components. Each subtask functions as an independent unit of work with its own assignee and due date, while remaining linked to the parent task for aggregated progress tracking.

For managers handling large volumes of work, our **Bulk Actions** in the list view are a major time-saver. You can select multiple tasks simultaneously and update their status, assignee, priority, or due date with just a few clicks. This is particularly useful during sprint planning or weekly project reviews. **Task Dependencies** allow you to model complex relationships between tasks. By linking tasks together, you can ensure that work proceeds in the correct sequence. For instance, you can set a 'Finish-to-Start' dependency, meaning Task B cannot begin until Task A is completed. This relationship is visually represented in the Gantt chart view, where moving one task will automatically adjust dependent tasks' schedules.

Categorization is handled through a flexible system of **Labels and Tags**. You can create workspace-wide labels for common categories like 'Bug', 'Feature', or 'Marketing', or project-specific tags for more granular tracking. The **Comments Section** within each task serves as a centralized hub for all task-related communication. Instead of losing context in long email threads or scattered chat messages, all discussions, feedback, and decisions remain attached to the task itself, creating a permanent audit trail and easing the onboarding of new team members to an ongoing project. You can @mention team members in comments to notify them directly and ensure timely responses.

## Project Views
TechFlow Pro recognizes that different people work in different ways, and different projects require different perspectives. We provide a suite of powerful project views that allow you to visualize your data exactly how you need it.

The **Board View** is our signature visualization, inspired by the Kanban methodology. It presents your tasks as cards organized into vertical columns representing different stages of your workflow. This view is highly interactive; you can drag and drop cards to change their status, providing an immediate sense of progress and flow. Columns can be customized to match your team's unique process, whether it's a simple 'To-Do' to 'Done' or a complex multi-stage QA pipeline.

The **List View** offers a more structured, spreadsheet-like interface. It is optimized for high-density information display and rapid data entry. In list view, you can see multiple task attributes at a glance—priority, assignee, due date, and labels—and use our advanced sorting and filtering tools to find exactly what you're looking for. This view is preferred by users who manage hundreds of tasks and need to perform frequent bulk edits.

The **Calendar View** maps your tasks onto a monthly or weekly calendar, providing a clear temporal overview of your team's workload. It is perfect for identifying deadline clusters and ensuring that your team's schedule is balanced. You can drag tasks to new dates directly on the calendar to reschedule them instantly.

For complex project planning and timeline management, the **Gantt Chart View** is an essential tool. It provides a horizontal bar chart that represents task durations and the dependencies between them. This view allows you to see the 'critical path' of your project and understand how delays in one area will impact the overall delivery date. It's the go-to view for project managers overseeing long-term initiatives with many moving parts.

The **Table View** (available in our Professional and Enterprise tiers) provides an even more customizable grid interface where you can hide or show columns, change their order, and use advanced formulas to calculate project metrics in real-time. Each project view can be refined using our **Advanced Filtering** engine. You can filter by any task attribute, including custom fields, and save these filters as 'Private Views' for your personal use or 'Shared Views' for your entire team. This ensures that everyone stays aligned and focused on the work that matters most.

## Integrations
TechFlow Pro is designed to be the central hub of your productivity ecosystem. We believe that your project management tool should play well with the rest of your tech stack, which is why we offer a wide range of deep, native integrations.

Our **Slack Integration** brings TechFlow Pro directly into your team's communication flow. Once connected, you can choose to have real-time notifications sent to specific Slack channels whenever a task is created, updated, or completed. This keeps everyone informed without them needing to constantly check the TechFlow app. Furthermore, you can use Slack commands like `/techflow create` to capture new ideas as tasks without leaving your conversation. You can also 'unfurl' TechFlow links in Slack to see a rich preview of task details.

For engineering teams, the **GitHub and GitLab Integrations** are game-changers. By linking your repositories to TechFlow Pro projects, you can automate your workflow. For example, you can configure TechFlow to automatically move a task to 'In Review' when a corresponding pull request is opened, and to 'Done' when that PR is merged. You can also see commit history and branch status directly within the TechFlow task pane, providing full visibility for both developers and project managers.

Our **Google Drive and Dropbox Integrations** allow you to attach files from your cloud storage directly to your tasks. Instead of uploading static copies, you're linking to the live documents. This ensures that everyone is always looking at the most up-to-date version and respects the permissions you've already set in your file storage system.

The **Zapier and Make.com Integrations** open up endless possibilities for automation. With Zapier, you can connect TechFlow Pro to over 5,000 other applications. You could create an automation that generates a TechFlow task whenever a new lead is captured in your CRM, or send a message to a Discord channel when a project milestone is reached.

For organizations with custom needs, TechFlow Pro offers a robust, well-documented **REST API**. Our API allows you to programmatically perform almost any action available in the UI, from creating projects and tasks to managing users and workspace settings. It uses standard HTTP methods and returns JSON, making it easy to integrate with your internal tools or build custom extensions. We also provide a comprehensive **Webhooks System**, which can push real-time data to your external applications whenever specific events occur in TechFlow Pro, such as task updates or project archival.

## Reporting and Analytics
TechFlow Pro doesn't just help you manage work; it provides the insights you need to improve how your team works. Our reporting and analytics suite turns your project data into actionable intelligence.

**Productivity Reports** provide a high-level view of team performance. You can see the number of tasks completed by each team member over a selected period, allowing you to identify your top contributors and those who may need additional support or resource rebalancing. These reports can be filtered by project, label, or priority to give you a granular understanding of where your team's effort is being spent.

For agile teams, our **Burndown and Burnup Charts** are essential for tracking progress during a sprint or project phase. A burndown chart shows the remaining work against the time available, helping you predict whether you'll meet your deadline. A burnup chart shows the total work completed against the total scope, which is useful for visualizing scope creep and overall project velocity.

The **Time Tracking Report** aggregates all the time entries made by your team members. This is invaluable for professional services organizations that need to bill clients based on hours worked, or for internal teams looking to understand the true cost of their projects. Time entries can be categorized by activity type and exported to CSV or integrated with popular accounting software.

**Custom Dashboards** allow you to create a personalized command center for your workspace. You can add various widgets to your dashboard to see key performance indicators (KPIs) at a glance. Widgets can show things like total overdue tasks, upcoming milestones, recent activity feeds, or charts representing task distribution by status or priority. Dashboards can be kept private or shared with your entire workspace to ensure everyone is focused on the same goals.

All data within TechFlow Pro belongs to you, and our **Advanced Export Tools** make it easy to take that data wherever you need it. You can export any project view or report to CSV, Excel, or PDF format. This is perfect for creating board-level reports, performing custom data analysis, or maintaining offline records for compliance purposes. Our export engine is fast and handles large datasets with ease, ensuring you always have access to your information.

## User Management and Security
Security and control are paramount when managing sensitive project data. TechFlow Pro provides a robust user management system and enterprise-grade security features to protect your information and ensure that the right people have the right level of access.

TechFlow Pro uses a **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)** model. There are four primary roles within a workspace:
- **Workspace Owner**: This is the highest level of authority. Owners have full access to all workspace settings, billing information, and user management. They are the only ones who can delete a workspace or transfer ownership.
- **Admin**: Admins have extensive permissions, including the ability to create and manage all projects, invite and remove users, and change workspace-wide settings. However, they cannot access billing or delete the workspace.
- **Member**: Members are the primary users of TechFlow Pro. They can create, edit, and delete tasks within projects to which they have been granted access. They can also create their own projects.
- **Viewer**: Viewers have read-only access to specific projects. They can see tasks, comments, and attachments but cannot make any changes. This role is perfect for sharing project progress with external stakeholders or clients.

Permissions can be further refined at the **Project Level**. You can make a project 'Public' to everyone in your workspace, or 'Private' and only accessible to specific invited members. This allows you to manage multiple teams and departments within a single workspace while maintaining strict data silos where necessary.

For our Enterprise customers, we offer **Single Sign-On (SSO)** via SAML 2.0. This allows your team to log in to TechFlow Pro using your existing identity provider, such as Okta, Azure AD, or Google Workspace. SSO simplifies user management for IT departments and enhances security by centralizing authentication. We also provide **Advanced Audit Logs**, which capture every significant action taken within your workspace, including user logins, permission changes, and data exports. These logs are essential for compliance and security investigations.

Data security is built into every layer of TechFlow Pro. All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher. We conduct regular third-party security audits and penetration tests to ensure our systems remain resilient against emerging threats. We are also proud to be **SOC 2 Type II Compliant**, demonstrating our commitment to the highest standards of security, availability, and confidentiality. Every user can (and should) enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** via an authenticator app, adding an essential second layer of protection to their account.

## Common How-Tos and Troubleshooting

### Password Reset Procedure
If you ever find yourself unable to log in, don't worry. On the login screen, simply click the 'Forgot Password?' link. Enter the email address associated with your TechFlow Pro account, and we will immediately send you an email containing a secure password reset link. Click the link within one hour to be taken to a page where you can set a new password. For security reasons, your new password must be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Once you've set your new password, you'll be automatically logged in and can resume your work.

### Updating Billing Information
To keep your subscription active and your team productive, it's important to keep your billing information up to date. Only a Workspace Owner can access these settings. Navigate to 'Workspace Settings' by clicking the gear icon in the sidebar, then select the 'Billing' tab. Here, you can see your current plan, view past invoices, and update your credit card or billing address. Any changes made to your subscription plan will be reflected in your next billing cycle. If you're on an annual plan, you can also manage your seats and renewal options from this screen.

### Performing a Data Export
Whether for a security audit or internal analysis, you can export your TechFlow Pro data at any time. Go to 'Workspace Settings', select 'Data Management', and then click 'Request Export'. We will package all your tasks, projects, comments, and file metadata into a secure ZIP file containing several CSV files. Once the export is ready, we'll send a download link to your email. For security, this link will remain active for only 24 hours.

### Generating API Keys
To use the TechFlow Pro API or set up custom integrations, you'll need an API key. Click on your avatar in the bottom-left corner and select 'Personal Profile'. Go to the 'Developer Settings' tab and click 'Generate New Token'. Give your token a descriptive name (e.g., 'Internal Reporting Script') and select the scopes it should have access to. **Important**: Once the token is generated, it will be shown to you only once. Copy it immediately and store it in a secure password manager. Never share your API keys or commit them to public code repositories.

### Webhook Configuration
Setting up webhooks is a great way to build reactive integrations. In any project, go to 'Project Settings', then 'Integrations', and select 'Webhooks'. Click 'Add Webhook' and enter the payload URL of your receiving application. You can then select exactly which events should trigger the webhook—for example, you might only want to be notified when a task is moved to the 'Done' column. We recommend setting a 'Webhook Secret' to verify that the payloads you receive are actually coming from TechFlow Pro.

### General Troubleshooting Steps
If you encounter any issues while using TechFlow Pro, we recommend the following steps:
1. **Refresh Your Browser**: Many minor UI or sync issues can be resolved by a simple page refresh.
2. **Check Your Connection**: Ensure you have a stable internet connection. TechFlow Pro requires a persistent connection to keep your data in sync.
3. **Browser Compatibility**: We recommend using the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. If you're using an older browser or a less common one, try switching to a supported browser to see if the issue persists.
4. **Clear Cache and Cookies**: Sometimes, stale browser data can cause unexpected behavior. Clearing your cache and cookies for techflow.io can often help.
5. **Disable Extensions**: Browser extensions, particularly ad-blockers or script-blockers, can sometimes interfere with TechFlow Pro's functionality. Try disabling your extensions to see if it resolves the problem.

## API Documentation and Reference
The TechFlow Pro API is a powerful tool for extending the platform's capabilities. It follows standard RESTful principles and is designed to be easy to use for developers of all skill levels.

### Authentication and Security
All API requests must be authenticated using a Bearer token in the 'Authorization' header. 
`Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY`
All requests must be made over HTTPS. Requests made over plain HTTP will be redirected or rejected.

### Rate Limiting and Quotas
To ensure fair usage and system stability, we enforce rate limits on all API accounts.
- **Professional Plan**: 1,000 requests per hour.
- **Enterprise Plan**: 5,000 requests per hour (can be increased upon request).
If you exceed your rate limit, the API will return a `429 Too Many Requests` status code. The `X-RateLimit-Reset` header will tell you when you can resume making requests.

### Core Endpoints and Methods
- `GET /v1/workspaces`: List all workspaces you belong to.
- `GET /v1/projects`: List all projects in a given workspace.
- `POST /v1/projects`: Create a new project.
- `GET /v1/tasks`: List tasks within a project. Supports filtering by status, assignee, and due date.
- `POST /v1/tasks`: Create a new task. Requires a `project_id` and a `title`.
- `GET /v1/tasks/{id}`: Retrieve full details for a specific task, including comments and subtasks.
- `PATCH /v1/tasks/{id}`: Update one or more attributes of a task.
- `DELETE /v1/tasks/{id}`: Archive a task (tasks are not permanently deleted, they are moved to an 'Archived' state).

### Error Handling and Status Codes
The TechFlow Pro API uses standard HTTP status codes to indicate the success or failure of a request.
- `200 OK`: The request was successful.
- `201 Created`: A new resource was successfully created.
- `400 Bad Request`: The request was malformed or missing required parameters. The response body will contain more details.
- `401 Unauthorized`: Authentication failed. Check your API key.
- `403 Forbidden`: You do not have permission to perform the requested action on this resource.
- `404 Not Found`: The requested resource does not exist.
- `429 Too Many Requests`: You have exceeded your API rate limit.
- `500 Internal Server Error`: An unexpected error occurred on our end. If this persists, please contact support.

For more detailed information, including request and response schemas and interactive examples, please visit our full developer portal at developers.techflow.io.

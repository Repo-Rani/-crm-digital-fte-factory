# TechFlow Company Profile

## Company Overview
TechFlow is a premier B2B SaaS company specializing in streamlined project management solutions for high-growth teams. Founded in 2018 and headquartered in San Francisco, TechFlow has rapidly scaled to support over 5,000 global customers, ranging from ambitious startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. With a dedicated team of 120 employees and a successful Series B funding round of $45 million led by Peak Venture Partners, TechFlow is committed to redefining how modern teams collaborate and deliver results.

## Product: TechFlow Pro
Our flagship product, TechFlow Pro, is an all-in-one project management and team collaboration platform. It combines task tracking, resource allocation, real-time communication, and advanced reporting into a single, intuitive interface. TechFlow Pro is designed for product managers, engineering leads, and operations directors who need a source of truth for their complex workflows.

## Pricing Plans
TechFlow offers flexible pricing designed to scale with your team's needs:
- **Starter Plan**: $29/month. Includes up to 5 users, basic task boards, and standard integrations.
- **Professional Plan**: $79/month. Includes up to 20 users, Gantt charts, time tracking, and priority support.
- **Enterprise Plan**: Custom pricing. Unlimited users, SSO/SAML, advanced security audit logs, and a dedicated success manager.

## Support Service Level Agreements (SLAs)
We pride ourselves on responsive support. Our targets for initial response times are:
- **Starter**: Within 48 hours.
- **Professional**: Within 24 hours.
- **Enterprise**: Within 4 hours (24/7).

## Key Facts & Infrastructure
- **Infrastructure**: Hosted on AWS us-east-1 for maximum reliability and global reach.
- **Security**: SOC2 Type II compliant. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit (AES-256).
- **Reliability**: 99.9% guaranteed uptime.
- **API Rate Limits**: 1,000 requests per hour for Professional/Enterprise tiers.

## Refund Policy
